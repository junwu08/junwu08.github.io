About

Jun Wu received the Ph.D. degree from Southern University of Science and Technology, Shenzhen, China, 2026, under the supervision of Dr. Weijie Yuan. His research interests include integrated sensing and communication, OTFS-assisted sensing and communication, low-altitude wireless networks, non-terrestrial networks, cooperative tracking, trajectory design, and control-aware wireless resource allocation.

He has worked on ISAC signal processing, UAV communications, OTFS-ISAC precoding, cooperative sensing, and networked control for low-altitude wireless systems.
