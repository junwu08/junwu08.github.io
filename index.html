About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Southern University of Science and Technology, supervised by Dr. Weijie Yuan. My work aims to bridge wireless signal processing, integrated sensing and communication, and networked control for intelligent low-altitude and non-terrestrial networks.

My recent research includes control-aware ISAC system design for UAV formations, OTFS-based ISAC waveform and precoder design, cooperative tracking and communication in low-altitude wireless networks, and resource-efficient wireless control under practical communication constraints.
