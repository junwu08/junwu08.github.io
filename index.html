About

Biography

Jun Wu is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at Southern University of Science and Technology, Shenzhen, China, under the supervision of Dr. Weijie Yuan. He received the B.E. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2021. He has also been a visiting student at Xi'an Jiaotong University and Sungkyunkwan University.

His current research lies at the intersection of wireless communications, signal processing, integrated sensing and communication, low-altitude wireless networks, and networked control.

Research Interests

Integrated Sensing and Communication

Beamforming, waveform design, sensing-assisted communication, and communication-aware sensing.

OTFS-ISAC

Delay-Doppler signal processing, OTFS waveform design, minimum-BER precoding, and SDR validation.

Low-Altitude Wireless Networks

UAV communications, cooperative tracking, trajectory design, resource allocation, and airspace management.

Networked and Robust Control

Model predictive control, networked control systems, formation control, and control-aware wireless design.

Convex and Non-Convex Optimization

Optimization methods for wireless communications, sensing, and control co-design problems.
